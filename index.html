Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any monetary prizes or prizes of monetary value?

Valentendo was created to help connect gamers with similar interests together in the hopes that many would find internet friends who they could game with outside of the event. In short: Due to currency differences outside of the U.S. & Canada, we will not be providing monetary prizes to people who participate in Valentendo.

Where is the Valentendo website located?

The website is located at valentendo.saturnmediagroup.ga.

Are you affiliated with Nintendo?

No.

Can I sponsor the event?

At this time, we currently do not have a program in place for this. This may change in the future, so keep an eye on this page. Thank you for your interest!
